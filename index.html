About Me

Howdy! My name is Louis Salamone. I'm born and raised in Ohio in a small river-side town that has a very small population, and eventually left home at 18 to attend Ohio University as an Audio Engineer and Music Indusrty major.

My time in ohio and especially at OU were fantastic - but upon graduating it was time for a new chapter - and i landed a job at a recording studio in Denver, CO, which is kind of all an ideal situation as i had a few friends out yonder and am super involved in the outdoors.

My audio involvemtn is primarily music recording as well as mixing and mastering - being lucky enough to work on projects all around the country with some amazing engineers. I feel very lucky for the life i have!
